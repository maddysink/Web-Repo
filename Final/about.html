About the Photographer

Hello! My name is Madeline Sinkovic. I am a Boulder, Colorado based photographer specializing in artistic photography and natural setting portraiture. I love to capture the world around me, especially different forms of water and its expression. I fell in love with photography my freshman year of high school, when I began taking classes for art. I have gained many years of experience since then, and have been operating Sinktography since March of 2020.

I grew up all over the world, moving every couple of years. From a very young age, I was exposed to many different, vibrant cultures and gained an appreciation for the world around me. I believe growing up overseas has made me the person and photographer I am today. My family now resides in Houston, Texas and I love to go visit... the food is amazing!

Currently, I am opperating out of Boulder, Colorado while I attend the University of Colorado studying Creative Technology and Design Engineering.
