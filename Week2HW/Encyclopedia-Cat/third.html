Care Instructions

There are many things that you must know to take care of your furry friend!

Food and Water

Cats need a combonation of both dry kibble food and wet canned food to live a happy and healthy life.

Additionally, cats enjoy drinking from running water, and often refuse to drink out of still water.

We recommend a flowing water fountain for your kitty!

Toys

There are many different toys you can buy for your cat. However, not all toys are created equal. Cats need

enriching and entertaining toys that will utilize their brains and keep them active. We have found puzzle toys and cat nip toys work best.

Sleep

Cats need a lot of sleep! In fact, most kitties sleep around 15 hours a day. Sometimes it can be frustrating

when your cat sleeps all day and parties all night, but cats are naturally nocturnal so there is little you can do.

Try playing extra with your cat right before bed to tire them out, or give them some kitty safe melatonin.
